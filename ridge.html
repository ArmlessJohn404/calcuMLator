Ridge Regression

The Ridge Regression is very similar to Linear Regression, but introduces a regularization term to avoid over-fitting the data, specially with higher order regressions. In our case this regularization does nothing. The coefficients are very similar to those of Linear Regression.

This method has a buitint cross validator, but the regualization in this case is useless.
